Bookshelf Hub's Book Store
Technology:

Native Android using Kotlin and Google Firebase.

Description:

Bookshelf Hub mobile app is a book store where individuals and students of any academic institution buy books that are in digital format. Books can also be non-academic books like Magazines, Newspapers, and more, simply any E-book in PDF format. Think of it as Google Play Store but for e-books.

Bookshelf Hub's Publishers Dashboard
Technology:

Angular and Google Firebase.

Description:

Bookshelf Hub's publisher's dashboard is a web application that is used by book publishers to publish their books for sale on the Bookshelf Hub book Store. Publishers can also have collaborators that sell the book on their behalf for a set commission by the publisher for each book sold by the collaborator.
